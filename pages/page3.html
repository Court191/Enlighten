Yoga

Yoga is the alignment of breath and body movement. In order to do well at both, you need to focus on your breath first then progress to the videos below. Generally, knowing how to do Sun Salutations is key in yoga. Here is a demonstration of how to do them. Make sure you warm up gently before and jump right in.

How to get started!

After you have mastered the sun salutations, we would suggest doing 8 on each side to really limber those joints and get the heart going, proceed onto some fun inversions.

You made it!

Well done for sticking with us! Now you can step it up a notch and try the static challenges such as the crow. Try to focus on a point in front of you and observe your breath before attempting it.

Experiencing Meditation

Once you have tried this level we would suggest practicing these for two to three weeks before going onto the harder ones.

Tips and Techniques for Beginners

Breathe, breathe, breathe. Keep relaxing and even on the days you don't feel like practicing, stand at the top of your mat and you will want to move in some way. You'll surprise yourself.
